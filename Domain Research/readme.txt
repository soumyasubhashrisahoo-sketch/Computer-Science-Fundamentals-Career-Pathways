IoT Domain Research & Career Mapping

Overview

This repository serves as a centralized portfolio for my academic and professional research into the Internet of Things (IoT). It documents my exploration of the domain, covering technological architectures, industrial applications, and career opportunities within the connected ecosystem.

Repository Contents

* INTERNET OF THING (IoT).docx

A detailed research report and career mapping document. This file contains:

Domain Analysis: A comprehensive look at how IoT bridges the physical and digital worlds.

Technology Stack: Breakdown of critical layers including Perception (Sensors), Connectivity (5G, Wi-Fi, Zigbee), and Cloud/Edge computing.

Career Mapping: Insights into roles such as IoT Solutions Architect and Embedded Systems Engineer, along with required skill sets.

Personal Reflection: My self-assessment of skills in Python, C++, and networking, aligned with a professional development plan.

* DOMAIN RESEARCH - INTERNET OF THINGS .pptx

A presentation deck summarizing the core research findings. It provides a visual overview of:

The 4-layer IoT Architecture.

Key Use Cases: Smart Cities, Industrial IoT (IIoT), and Healthcare (IoMT).

Future trends and strategic technologies shaping the industry.

Objectives

The primary goals of this repository are to:

Demonstrate a solid understanding of the IoT landscape.

Identify and analyze the key protocols (MQTT, CoAP) and platforms (AWS IoT, Azure) essential for the industry.

Showcase the alignment between my academic background and professional aspirations in smart infrastructure and automation.
